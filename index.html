About Martha

Welcome to Martha Barnds Art, your destination for original watercolor paintings, prints and ceramics. Martha is a Missouri‑based artist whose work is inspired by nature, architecture and the simple pleasures of everyday life. Each piece is lovingly crafted to capture the warmth and character of its subject.

Whether you're looking for a custom pet portrait, a house painting or a unique ceramic piece, Martha brings your vision to life with vibrant colours and delicate brushwork. Browse our collection of available prints or commission a one‑of‑a‑kind artwork tailored to you.
