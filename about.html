About Martha

Martha Barnds is a self‑taught watercolor artist based in Lake Saint Louis, Missouri. From an early age she found joy in transforming blank paper into vibrant scenes. Her passion led her to explore various mediums, but watercolor remains her favourite for its luminous transparency and unpredictable nature.

Martha draws inspiration from the world around her – treasured family homes, beloved pets and the natural beauty of the Midwest. When she isn’t painting, she enjoys teaching workshops and sharing her love for art with the community. Through each commission and print she hopes to create meaningful keepsakes that will be cherished for years.

Thank you for supporting handmade art. Your purchases and commissions directly help a local artist continue doing what she loves.
